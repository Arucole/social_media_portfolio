Women In Abuja

Overview

Women In Abuja is a brand connecting businesses, sellers to buyers, buyers to sellers and equally running adverts & promotions as well as jobs/business opportunity. This brand was created to mainly as a community to grow businesses, events and community initiatives.

The goal of this project was to increase brand awareness and boost reach to raise visibility and far reaching digital impact over the period of 4 years.

December 2019 - February 2023

My Role

Social Media Manager, Content Manager, Community Manager, Brand Management, Micro-influencer Management, Community Marketing, User-Generated Content Management.

Background

The business brand faced the problem of reaching the general public. The inability to archive desired goals and objectives as a result of no visibility and inability to connect to the general public was a problem. The Covid-19 times offered the opportunity for businesses to herness the benefits of Social Media like never before fostering a strong online presence. This was why I wanted to tackle the challenge.

I joined a team to manage the brands presence across different social media platforms. The group and I reported to the owner of the business and was overseen by the owner. I was responsible for the social media management and user-generated content management.

While the experience was long, I was extremely fortunate for the experience and I got to implement my strategy of community building and management organically and via sponsored adverts. Some key achievements are listed below;

  • Implemented a successful strategy to solve the problem. I was able to experience first-hand the implementation of a process to solve a problem presented. Initiated meetings with the business owner brought forward the problem of the brand awareness and reach. I was involved in the process of strategy formation by brainstorming through consistent WhatsApp messaging. liasing with the business owner and weekly meetings & analysis.
  • Established a collaborative team-working process. I was able to levrage my previous social media experience and extensive academic knowledge while also learning from the other members. working within a collaborative, highly creative and innovative team was truely an amazing experience
  • Developed trusting relationships with owner. Throughout the experience I worked closely with the business owner. I showcased Consistency and dependability and developed a professional and trusting relationship.

Understanding The Problem

I actively listened, asked questions and took notes in the first initial meeting (2 weeks in) with the business owner and I concluded the problem faced were low followership and social media presence, brand awareness and instagram reach.

Using my experience I suggested the following;

  • The power of social Media grew exponetially and online traffic increased worldwide due to lockdown. This meant there was opportunity for growth and success for the brand awareness.
  • Leverage partnerships and sponsored adverts for exclusive special offers increasing reach greatly(1,000 - 15,000 followers) building trust for the brand
  • User-generated content encourages followers to interact and engage while also encouraging others to share content.

Strategic Approach

Some strategies used to increase event awareness included;

  • Using a multi-channel approach of various digital platforms via sponsored and organic growth across muitiple social media platforms.
  • A consistent build-up with multiple post a day on instagram feeds, consistently posting stories and posting similar content on other social media channels of the brand.
  • Providing valuable content, engaging the generally public and creating value.
  • Using micro-influencers from different regions to promote the instagram page.
  • Actively responding to and re-sharing participants content overtime.

Some strategies used to boost Instagram reach included;

  • A second competition to win prizes
  • User-generated content through @mentions, tagging and use of event #'s increased reach.
  • Video content to boost engagement and reach.
  • After post tag and share interactions in stories building community interactions.
